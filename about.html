About Our Church

Welcome to MFM CAASO Youth Church, Upperroom Tabernacle. We are a vibrant community committed to prayer, the Word of God, and raising a generation of youths who are passionate about God's kingdom.

Our Story Our Leadership
Our Journey

Our Story

MFM CAASO Youth Church was established with a divine mandate to raise a generation of fire-brand youths who are spiritually grounded, academic/career giants, and agents of transformation in their society.

What began as a small gathering of young people hungry for God's Word has grown into a vibrant community impacting lives across Lagos and beyond. Through prayer, worship, and discipleship, we've witnessed countless lives transformed by the power of God.

Our church is part of the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries (MFM) worldwide, operating under the CAASO Region 17 Headquarters. We remain committed to our core values of prayer, holiness, excellence, and community transformation.

Mission & Vision

The driving force behind everything we do at MFM CAASO Youth Church.

Our Mission

To raise a generation of fire-brand youths who are spiritually grounded, academic/career giants, and agents of transformation in their society.

Our Vision

To be the leading youth church that produces world-changers through the power of prayer, holiness, and excellence.

Our Core Values

What We Believe

Our Beliefs

The fundamental truths that guide our faith and practice.

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God, providing complete guidance for faith and life.

Salvation

We believe in salvation through faith in Jesus Christ alone, by God's grace, resulting in eternal life and transformation.

Holy Spirit

We believe in the present ministry of the Holy Spirit, whose indwelling empowers Christians for godly living and service.

The Church

We believe in the universal church as the body of Christ, and the local church as a community of believers for worship, fellowship, and ministry.

Our History

Church Timeline

Key milestones in our journey of faith and growth.

2015

Foundation Laid

MFM CAASO Youth Church was officially inaugurated with 25 founding members committed to youth ministry.

2017

First Youth Camp

Organized our first annual youth camp, attracting over 100 young people from across Lagos.

2019

Church Growth

Membership grew to over 200 active youth members. Launched various ministry departments.

2022

Digital Expansion

Launched online services and digital discipleship programs, reaching youth across Nigeria and beyond.

2024

New Beginnings

Expanded our outreach programs and launched community transformation initiatives across Lagos.
